BUZZ VS.
THE WORLD

THE GAME :

You are Georgia Tech's mascot, Buzz! Defend your honor from rival mascots by shooting them with your stinger before they can attack.

HOW TO PLAY :

Avoid getting hit by rival mascots! Left click with your cursor at any spot on the screen to send a projectile in that direction. You gain 50 points for each opponent you defeat. Get hit, and you lose.
You can make the game easier by maximizing your screen or harder by minimizing it.
